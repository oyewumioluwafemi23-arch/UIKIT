OUR STORY

Built by Students, For Students

We understand the late-night study sessions, the exam anxiety, and the balancing act of social life. Clear Sky isn't just a platform; it's a community-driven response to the mental health challenges in higher education.

Our goal is to provide accessible, jargon-free resources that empower you to take control of your mental well-being.

What Drives Us

Radical Empathy

We don't just provide tips; we provide understanding. Every resource is vetted by peers who have been where you are.

Privacy First

Your journey is your own. We ensure your data and your discussions remain strictly confidential and secure.

Future Ready

We use modern AI and data tools responsibly to help predict stress triggers and provide proactive support.
